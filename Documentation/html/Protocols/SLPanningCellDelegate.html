SLPanningCellDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in SLPanningCell.h

Overview

Classes that conform to SLPanningCellDelegate may receive messages about the state of panning cells.

Note that the “will” methods won’t fire unless it’s absolutely certain that the cell will end up in that state. That is, if a pan gesture opens the cell most of the way, then closes it most of the way, then ends, the only calls made to the delegate will be cellWillPanClosed: and cellDidPanClosed:.

Tasks

  • – cellWillPanOpen:

    Fires when the cell is about to start its animation towards being open.
  • – cellDidPanOpen:

  • – cellWillPanClosed:

    Fires when the cell is about to start its animation towards being closed.
  • – cellDidPanClosed:

    Fires when the cell has completed its animation towards being closed.
